CMF by Nothing Watch Pro: The Perfect Blend of Style and Functionality

Looking for a feature-packed smartwatch that combines aesthetics with cutting-edge technology? The CMF by Nothing Watch Pro might just be your perfect match! With its 1.96-inch AMOLED display, advanced health tracking features, and Bluetooth calling, this smartwatch promises to elevate your lifestyle.

Key Highlights

  • A 1.96-inch AMOLED screen with 410x502 resolution ensures vivid colors and sharp visuals.
  • 600 nits peak brightness makes it easy to view under bright sunlight.
  • Enjoy 58 fps refresh rate for ultra-smooth touch responsiveness.
  • Make and receive calls directly from your smartwatch.
  • AI noise cancellation ensures crystal-clear conversations, even in noisy environments.
  • Equipped with 5 satellite positioning systems, offering unmatched accuracy for outdoor activities like running, hiking, and cycling.
  • Real-time tracking for
  • Heart rate
  • SpO2 (blood oxygen levels)
  • Sleep and stress levels

Features You’ll Love

  • Track calories burned, steps, distance, and more.
  • Set personalized goals and watch your progress grow.
  • Stay updated with smart notifications for calls, texts, emails, and app alerts.
Premium Build & Design
  • Ash grey silicone strap and a metal-aluminum alloy dialfor a sleek, lightweight design.
  • Water-resistant,making it perfect for outdoor activities.
  • Bluetooth 5.3 ensures a stable and fast connection.
  • Compatible with both Android (5.0+) and iOS (5.0+) devices.
