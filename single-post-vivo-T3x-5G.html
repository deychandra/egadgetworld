Vivo T3x 5G Smartphone

The Vivo T3x 5G is here, packed with advanced features and cutting-edge technology, making it a formidable contender in the mid-range smartphone segment. Here’s everything you need to know about this powerful device.

Key Specifications:

  • RAM & Storage:4 GB RAM | 128 GB ROM | Expandable Up to 1 TB
  • Display: 17.06 cm (6.71-inch) Full HD+ LCD Display
  • Cameras: 50MP + 2MP Dual Rear Cameras | 8MP Front Camera
  • Battery: 6000 mAh with 44W Fast Charging
  • Processor: Snapdragon 6 Gen 1 (4 nm technology)
  • Operating System: Android 14 (Funtouch OS 14)
  • Design: Sleek, vibrant, and durable with IP64 dust and water resistance

The Vivo T3x 5G comes in the following variants:

  • 4 GB RAM + 128 GB ROM
  • 8 GB RAM + 128 GB ROM (with 8 GB Extended RAM)

Both variants support storage expansion up to 1 TB via a hybrid slot, ensuring ample space for your apps, photos, and videos.

Product Highlights:
  • Powerful Performance The Vivo T3x 5G is powered by the Snapdragon 6 Gen 1 5G mobile platform, built on a 4 nm architecture. With an octa-core CPU and a benchmark score of approximately 560K+, this processor ensures smooth gaming, seamless multitasking, and lag-free performance.
  • Immersive Display Enjoy a cinematic experience with the 120 Hz Full HD+ LCD display. The punch-hole design, wide color saturation (83% NTSC), and local peak brightness of 1000 nits make content vibrant and engaging. Whether you're gaming, streaming, or browsing, this 6.71-inch display delivers stunning visuals.
  • Long-lasting Battery With a massive 6000 mAh battery, the Vivo T3x ensures that you stay connected all day. It supports 44W fast charging, allowing you to power up quickly and get back to your activities without delays.
  • Enhanced Audio ExperienceThe dual stereo speakers with Audio Booster technology deliver a cinematic audio experience. Whether you’re watching movies or listening to music, the sound is clear, immersive, and impactful.
  • Exceptional Camera System Rear Camera: The 50MP primary sensor captures stunningly detailed photos even in low light. Features like Super Night Mode and 4K video recording enhance your photography and videography experience. Front Camera: The 8MP front-facing camera ensures sharp and vibrant selfies with features like Portrait Mode and Live Photo.
  • Vibrant Design The Vivo T3x sports a premium design with a high-end “premium dial” camera layout. The slim profile (7.99 mm) and lightweight build (199 g) make it comfortable to hold and visually appealing.
  • Extended RAM for Seamless Multitasking The 8 GB RAM variant supports Extended RAM technology, adding up to 8 GB of virtual memory. This allows for smoother switching between apps and running multiple tasks simultaneously.
  • Rugged and Reliable The Vivo T3x is IP64-rated for dust and water resistance, providing added durability. The phone’s robust design ensures it can withstand everyday challenges with ease.

In the Box:

  • Vivo T3x 5G Handset
  • Quick Start Guide
  • USB Cable
  • Charger
  • Eject Tool
  • Protective Case
  • Pre-applied Screen Protector
  • Warranty Card
