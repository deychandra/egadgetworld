realme P1 5G (Peacock Green, 256 GB, 8 GB RAM) – The Ultimate Smartphone Experience

Looking for a smartphone that combines style, performance, and value? The realme P1 5G in stunning Peacock Green is your perfect match. With a host of top-notch features, it’s built to impress tech enthusiasts and everyday users alike.

Key Specifications of realme P1 5G

  • Display: 16.94 cm (6.67 inch) Full HD+ AMOLED Display
  • Processor: MediaTek Dimensity 7050 Chipset
  • RAM & Storage: 8 GB RAM | 256 GB ROM (Expandable up to 2 TB)
  • Cameras:

    • Front: 16MP
  • Battery: 5000 mAh with 45W Supervooc Fast Charging
  • Operating System: Android 14 with Realme UI 5.0

Why Choose realme P1 5G?

1. Unmatched Performance

The MediaTek Dimensity 7050 5G chipset ensures seamless multitasking, fast app launches, and smooth gameplay. Coupled with Smart 5G features, you can enjoy blazing-fast internet speeds without interruptions.

2. Immersive AMOLED Display

The 120Hz refresh rate on the 6.67-inch Full HD+ AMOLED screen makes scrolling and gaming buttery smooth. Features like in-display fingerprint scanning and Sunlight Screen technology enhance usability outdoors.

3. Stunning Camera Capabilities

The 50MP primary camera delivers sharp and detailed photos, while the 2MP mono lens adds depth. The 16MP front camera is perfect for selfies and video calls, with features like Night Mode and AI enhancements.

4. Long-Lasting Battery Life

Stay powered all day with the 5000 mAh battery, supported by 45W fast charging. Reverse charging adds extra convenience, letting you charge other devices on the go.

5. Premium Build & Design

With a sleek 7.97mm design and weighing just 188g, the realme P1 5G is stylish and easy to handle. Its Peacock Green finish exudes elegance, making it stand out.
