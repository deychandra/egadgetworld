CMF by Nothing Buds 42 dB Active Noise Cancellation, Ultra Bass Technology, 35.5 Hrs playtime Bluetooth Gaming (Orange, True Wireless)

The CMF by Nothing Buds are a game-changer for audiophiles and gamers alike, offering cutting-edge technology wrapped in a sleek, orange true wireless design. From 42 dB active noise cancellation to ultra bass technology, these earbuds are designed to deliver an unparalleled audio experience.

Key Features of CMF by Nothing Buds

  • Active Noise Cancellation (ANC): With 42 dB ANC, eliminate unwanted distractions and immerse yourself in crystal-clear sound.
  • Dynamic Drivers: Custom 12.4 mm bio-fiber diaphragm for high-definition audio clarity.
  • Ultra Bass Technology: Adjustable bass levels for deep, impactful sound tailored to your preferences.
  • Playtime: Up to 35.5 hours on a single charge, including the charging case.
  • Fast Charging: Just 10 minutes of charging provides up to 7 hours of playback—perfect for busy lifestyles.
  • Low Latency: Designed for seamless gaming with ultra-low latency audio performance.

Why Choose CMF by Nothing Buds?

1. Premium Audio Performance

With high-definition sound and customizable bass, these earbuds are perfect for music lovers and gamers.

2. All-Day Comfort

The lightweight design and water-resistant features make them suitable for any activity, whether it’s a gym session or a long day at work.

3. Advanced Technology

Powered by Bluetooth 5.3, these earbuds provide a seamless connection across devices like laptops, smartphones, and tablets.
